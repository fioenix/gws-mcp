# Code of Conduct

Short version: be a decent human in issues, PRs, and discussions. We care about the work, not posturing.

## Expected behaviour

- Stay technical. Argue the design, not the person.
- Assume good faith on the first round. Ask for clarification before escalating.
- Disagreement is fine and useful. Make it concrete — point at the line, the benchmark, the spec.
- Credit other people's ideas.

## Not welcome

- Personal attacks, harassment, doxxing.
- Spam, off-topic promotion, drive-by "you should rewrite this in $LANG" comments.
- Sustained hostility after a reasonable explanation has been given.

## Enforcement

The maintainer (@fioenix on GitHub) handles reports. If a participant is making the project worse, the maintainer may close threads, hide comments, or block the account. There is no formal committee — keep expectations proportional.

Report problems by opening a [private security advisory](https://github.com/fioenix/gws-mcp/security/advisories/new) if you want privacy, or by tagging the maintainer in the relevant issue.
